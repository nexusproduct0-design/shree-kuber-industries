Our Story

Trusted Garlic
Processor from MP

Shree KUBER Industries is one of India's premium peeled garlic manufacturers, established in Dalauda, Mandsaur — the garlic capital of Madhya Pradesh. Founded by Vinay Patidar and Tejas Patidar, we deliver farm-fresh garlic products to hotels, restaurants, food processors and wholesalers across India.

Our nitrogen-flush packaging technology, rigorous quality protocols, and nation-wide logistics make us the trusted choice for bulk garlic buyers — from cloud kitchens to industrial spice manufacturers.

🌾

Mandsaur is India's most prolific garlic-growing region — our proximity guarantees freshness no other supplier can match.
🏭

🚚
Nationwide Cold-Chain

Our Process

Farm to Your Doorstep

Six carefully engineered stages ensuring every clove that leaves our facility is clean, fresh, and perfectly sealed.

🌾
Step 01
Farm Selection
Only certified, premium garlic bulbs from Mandsaur's best farms are procured each season.
🧹
Step 02
Cleaning & Sorting
Bulbs are washed, de-rooted, and sorted by grade and size before any processing begins.
🧄
Step 03
Hygienic Peeling
Mechanical peeling in food-grade sterile environment — no chemicals, no contamination.
🔬
Step 04
Quality Check
Colour, aroma, size uniformity, and microbial testing on every batch without exception.
💨
Step 05
Nitrogen Packing
Nitrogen-flushed sealing locks freshness and extends shelf life to 60+ days naturally.
🚚
Step 06
Fast Dispatch
Same-day or next-day dispatch via cold-chain logistics to any city across India.
